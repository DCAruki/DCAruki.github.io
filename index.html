About Me

A dreamer who found home in stories

I'm Arukiya — a writer, digital artist, and hopeless romantic who believes the most profound emotions are the ones left unspoken. Romance anime taught me that vulnerability is a form of courage, and that the space between two people can hold entire universes.

When I'm not deep in a 24-episode emotional spiral, I'm writing, sketching, or building little digital worlds that feel like the opening scene of a slice-of-life anime — soft light, warm coffee, and the quiet hum of something beginning.

My Story

The Journey So Far

Chapters of a story still being written.

2019

The First Anime

Watched "Bunny Girl Senpai" for the first time and cried for three hours straight. Something shifted. I never looked back.

2020

100 Anime Milestone

Hit 100 completed anime. Realized romance was more than a genre — it was a lens for understanding people.

2022

Started Writing

Began writing short romance stories inspired by the anime that moved me most. Published the first one online.

2023

Discovered Digital Art

Started illustrating characters from my stories. Found a new language for emotion — one made of color and line.

2026

Built This Space

Decided to bring everything together — a little corner of the internet that feels like home. You're in it now.
